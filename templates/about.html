Social Network Analysis (SNA), the Heart of our Project:

The study of social networks is a multidisciplinary view of a problem that includes social, mathematical, statistical, and computer sciences. A social network analysis can help illustrate patterns and important parameters. Our social network for this project refers to a grouping of organizations that are involved in climate adaption and mitigation. Each of these institutions are represented as a node in a graph, and a social network analysis ties all the nodes together to create edges using network and graph theory.In our SNA, the edges are created from the affiliation type of the nodes. This could be science data, a funder, multiple relations, governance legality, or other.
